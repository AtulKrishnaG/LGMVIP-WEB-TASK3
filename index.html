Common Guidelines for all events

1. The following competitions are free and open to all.
2. Except cultural events all other events are based on the theme of “Engineering and Science”.
3. Make sure you are not disqualified due to deviating from the theme or unsatisfactory explanation to the jury.
4. All your queries will be solved and your entries were taken through Whatsapp Groups, Therefore, All the Participants are requested to join in the Whatsapp group prescribed in the application.
5. Selection : The contestant will be judged solely on the basis of submission and the final judgement is done by Team EngineersConnect.
6. E-Certificates will be given to all the participants of this event.
7. 3 winners will be selected on each competition and get their reward as Amazon gift vouchers.
8. Entries are accepted till 10-september-2020 (23:59:59).
9. Results will be announced on 15-september-2020.
